Where I Started

My career began in classrooms across Indonesia as an English teacher and IELTS/TOEFL tutor, where I developed a deep passion for helping learners gain confidence through language and education — designing Pearson and Cambridge-aligned curricula for 500+ learners across multiple institutions.

Where I Am Now

As Senior Instructional Design QA Specialist at The KPI Institute (Melbourne, remote), I architect 50+ course development guidelines, deliver quality reviews for 600+ course assets monthly, and lead a structured feedback framework adopted by 70+ instructional designers and copywriters worldwide.

What Drives Me

I bring a learner-centered approach combining ADDIE/SAM methodology, Bloom's Taxonomy, Kirkpatrick evaluation, and emerging AI tools — creating learning ecosystems that don't just inform, but genuinely empower people to grow, perform, and succeed.
