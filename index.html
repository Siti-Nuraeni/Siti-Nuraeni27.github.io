Background

Educator at Heart,
Designer by Practice

I'm Siti Nuraeni — an instructional designer and learning QA specialist with roots in English education and language teaching. My career began in the classroom as an English teacher and IELTS tutor, where I discovered my passion for helping learners grow in confidence and capability.

Over time, I transitioned into instructional design and quality assurance, working with government ministries, international universities, and global training organizations. Today I bring a learner-centered approach to every project — combining pedagogy, technology, and thoughtful design to create experiences that don't just inform, but empower.

Currently based in Jakarta, I'm working with The KPI Institute as a Senior Instructional Design QA Specialist, designing and reviewing e-learning content for global audiences.
